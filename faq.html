HOW DO I PLACE AN ORDER?

Click on the Order Now tab at the top of the page and fill out the form with all your requirements. When finished, click on the "Place Order" button at the bottom of the page and send the resulting e-mail to us. If you wish to order a display product, simply select the product(s) from the drop-down menus and place the order.

OR

Send us an e-mail or give us a call at least 48 hours prior to pick-up or delivery, with:

  • Your name and phone number
  • The item(s) you wish to order
  • The date, time, and location of pick-up or delivery

We will confirm your order by e-mail or phone as soon as possible.

CAN YOU DELIVER MY ORDER?

Yes, we offer delivery within the GTA for an additional fee of $10.00. Please contact us in advance so we can confirm that the date and time you have chosen is available.

I NEED A GIFT FOR TOMORROW/TONIGHT! CAN I PLACE A LAST-MINUTE ORDER?

Give us a call and we'll see what we can do. A late order fee of $5.00 will apply.

CAN I MAKE A CHANGE TO A PRODUCT?

Yes, all of our products are fully customizable. See our Custom Order page for a list of options. If you still don't see what you're looking for, contact us and we'll do our best to accomodate your request. Additional charges may apply.
